About Me

Back when I was a kid, I was all about being a detective or something like that. Then I got into the idea of writing a book, but I also thought being a businessman could be cool. I was into mechanical stuff, so engineering seemed like the obvious choice.

Along the way, I had a blast dabbling in crypto trading to make some extra cash and even looked into pilot courses. I got myself a DSLR to fulfill my photography dreams and played around with animations too. Even though I ended up studying aeronautical engineering, I never really saw myself as an engineer. I was more into the idea of managing stuff, so now I’m diving into Operations Management. It’s where my passion lies, and I’ve got some killer ideas brewing. But sales? Nah, not my thing, you feel me?
